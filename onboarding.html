How It Works

1

Navigate to a Legal Document

Visit any webpage containing a legal document, such as terms of service, privacy policy, or contract.

2

Click the Extension Icon

Click the Legal Document Analyzer icon in your Chrome toolbar to open the popup.

3

Analyze the Document

Click "Analyze Document" to start the analysis. The extension will scan the page content.

4

Review the Results

Explore the highlighted text, tooltips, and summary panel to better understand the document.

Tips & Customization

Use the Right-Click Menu

You can also right-click anywhere on a page and select "Analyze Legal Document" from the context menu.

Customize Your Experience

Visit the Settings page to customize appearance, analysis options, and advanced features.

Privacy-Focused

By default, all analysis happens locally in your browser. No document content is sent to external servers.

Toggle Features

You can enable or disable individual features like highlighting, simplification, and issue detection.
